The Dolphin Oasis

Welcome to the Dolphin Oasis, where we have been healing hundreds of injured dolphins from around our area. We have a database of information about our dolphins in a table labeled, dolphins with the following fields:

name
Each one has a story to tell
weight
in kilograms (actually its mass)
length
in meters
last-fed
The last time the dolphin had been fed. They are fed in the morning when our care-givers arrive at 5:00am, and should be fed after 4 hours, but no longer than every 6 hours.

Can you tell me how many dolphins we have at our oasis? 

SELECT COUNT(name) as dolphins FROM dolphins

Can you figure out the average weight and length of our dolphins, and with that information guess what kind of dolphins we care for? 

SELECT AVG(length) AS avg_length,
       AVG(weight) AS avg_weight FROM dolphins
